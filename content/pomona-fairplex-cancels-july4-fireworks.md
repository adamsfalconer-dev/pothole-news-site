> *Reported July 2026 as part of our founding archive — this account documents June 2026 events.*

Fairplex is sitting out the fireworks business this Independence Day. The Pomona fairgrounds announced it will not stage KABOOM!, its July 4 fireworks show, in 2026.

"Fairplex will not host KABOOM! in 2026 as we focus on new opportunities and experiences across our campus," the venue said in a statement.

Those new experiences have a name: cricket. The same announcement says Fairplex will host professional Major League Cricket matches July 1 through 5, including two matches on July 4 — the holiday slot the fireworks used to fill.

**Why it matters:** KABOOM! has been Fairplex's Fourth of July fireworks show, and its absence leaves a hole in Pomona's holiday calendar. What's stepping into that hole is telling: the fairgrounds is giving its marquee holiday weekend to ticketed professional cricket instead of a fireworks show — a concrete sign of how Fairplex is reprogramming its campus.

Fairplex offered no explanation beyond the pivot to "new opportunities," and named no other reason for dropping the show.
