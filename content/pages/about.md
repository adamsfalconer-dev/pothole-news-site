**Pothole News is an independent newsroom covering local government across the Pomona Valley — six communities in two counties: Pomona, Diamond Bar, Walnut, Chino Hills, Rowland Heights, and the City of Industry.**

Most of these places share almost no dedicated coverage of their city halls, school boards, water districts, and county governance. Decisions about your taxes, your police, your land, and your schools get made in half-empty meeting rooms and posted — when they're posted at all — in PDFs nobody reads. We exist to read them, connect them across the whole valley, and hand you what matters in a few minutes a week.

## What we do

- **Watch the meetings you don't have time to.** Every city council, planning commission, county board, and special district in our footprint — agendas, packets, votes, and the records behind them.
- **Follow the money and the land.** Contracts, budgets, campaign finance, and development — the recurring stories that only make sense when someone tracks them over time.
- **Keep the record.** When a decision happens and no one publishes the result, that gap *is* the story. We hold the standing record so accountability doesn't depend on who happened to be in the room.
- **Credit and connect other coverage.** When another outlet breaks it first, we say so, link them, and add what only a valley-wide view can. See our weekly **Worth your click**.

## How we work

We're a small operation that uses **AI tools under human editorial control** to monitor far more public meetings and records than a newsroom our size otherwise could. A human is responsible for every published word. We're transparent about exactly how that works on our [Ethics & Standards](/ethics/) page.

Our weekly roundup lands in your inbox free. **[Subscribe](/#/portal/signup)** — no cost, no ads, unsubscribe anytime.
